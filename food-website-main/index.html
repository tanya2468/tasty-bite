Daal Bati Churma

Dal Bati Churma is the most popular dish in Rajasthani cuisine. It is made of three components of bati, dal, and churma. Dal is lentils, bati is a baked wheat ball, and churma is powdered sweetened cereal. Churma is a popular delicacy usually served with baatis and dal. It is coarsely ground wheat crushed and cooked with ghee and sugar.

Traditionally it is made by mashing up wheat flour baatis or left over rotis in ghee and jaggery.

Gatte Ki Sabzi

Gatte ki sabzi, an authentic dish from Rajasthani cuisine is a delicious curry prepared with gram flour as base ingredient. Gatte means cooked gram flour dumplings, which are added to the spicy curd gravy to make this irresistible curry. It tastes best when served with roti or any delicious Indian flat bread, dal and plain rice in lunch
